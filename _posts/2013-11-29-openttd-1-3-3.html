--- title: OpenTTD 1.3.3 author: planetmaker ---

Here we present you our newest stable release: OpenTTD 1.3.3!

Compared to the previous release candidate an important crashy bug was fixed. We recommend everyone to update to this version, especially server owners are encouraged to update in a timely manner

Feedback is very much appreciated. Should you encounter any further bugs, please report them to our bug tracker.
Gameplay help can be found in our wiki, the forums or on IRC. Also visit forums or IRC for general discussions about the game and developments around it.

Enjoy,
Your OpenTTD team
