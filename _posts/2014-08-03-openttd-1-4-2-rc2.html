--- title: OpenTTD 1.4.2-RC2 author: Alberth --- Despite the sunny summer weather (or perhaps thanks to the warm weather), several people found new bugs in the previous release candidate.

As highlights, ships used the wrong sound for a break down, and there was a sudden loss of tractive effort due to an overflow with trains. Also, Slovak number separator locale was fixed. The complete list of fixes can be found at the Changelog.

Thanks to all who found and reported these bugs. It helps in making OpenTTD a stable program that can be enjoyed for many years.

For further testing, we are happy to present a new OpenTTD 1.4.2-RC2 version.

If you find any problems, please report them to our bug tracker.
Your OpenTTD team
