We're aware of some frustrating intermittent issues with some OpenTTD services, including:

* Multiplayer server listings
* Bananas website content listings
* Bananas login for content authors

We have documented the technical details of the issue here:
[OpenTTD/infra#3](https://github.com/OpenTTD/infra/issues/3)

We are investigating solutions. But, unfortunately, as a volunteer project we can't give a good estimate for long it will be until we can resolve this.
