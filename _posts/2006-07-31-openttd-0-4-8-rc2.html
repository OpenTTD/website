--- title: OpenTTD 0.4.8-RC2 author: Darkvater --- It seems some unfortunate organizational problems have prevented a swift release of RC2, dragging RC1 for a month instead of the supposed week.
RC1 had a nasty industry problem that prevented goods produced by them to be used. RC2 fixes this, along with a few other minor cornercases. Please test this version extensively, hopefully all is well.

As usual the files can be found in the downloads section and a longer announcement over at the forums.
