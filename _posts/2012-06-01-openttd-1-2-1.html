--- title: OpenTTD 1.2.1 author: frosch --- It's friday. If you like fish, you might be happy. If you don't, well, how about OpenTTD 1.2.1?

The first maintenance release of the 1.2 series delivers you a lot of bug fixes, so hopefully a nice playing experience. Most notable the lagging experienced in certain circumstances with 1.2.0 on the title screen and in-game should be gone.

You can find the download in the usual spot at http://www.openttd.org/download-stable, and also a detailed changelog next to it.

Have fun and a nice weekend playing OpenTTD!
