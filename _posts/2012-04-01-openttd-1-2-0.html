--- title: OpenTTD 1.2.0? author: planetmaker ---

As anticipated by many, we present you a new release today.

With this release we introduce a number of major fixes like issues with aircraft freezing mid-flight, time-tabled travel at 0 km/h, wrongly skipped sprites, imported sounds, or reversing trains. See the full changelog for details.

Have fun playing and testing, and please make sure that you report any bugs you encounter to our bug tracker.
