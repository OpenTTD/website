Third try's the charm? We hope so; here's our third release candidate for OpenTTD 15.

Most notably, the game no longer forgets your script configuration, and we've also fixed some rare crashes. See the changelog for the full list.

As always, if you spot any more bugs, please [report them here](https://github.com/OpenTTD/OpenTTD/issues/new/choose).

If you're one of our valued translators, this is the last call for translations for OpenTTD 15! Check on your language [here](https://translator.openttd.org/project/openttd-master).
