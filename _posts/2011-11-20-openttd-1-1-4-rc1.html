--- title: OpenTTD 1.1.4-RC1 author: frosch --- It's autumn in the north, and it's release time! To provide you with the best playing experience throughout the dark cold nights in the next months, we are preparing a new bug fix release. (If you are on the southern hemisphere, just go swimming or so :s )

The first release candidate for 1.1.4 provides fixes for bugs in various areas: NewGRF support, NoAI, GUI and savegame compatibility. Please help testing it, and report the bugs you encounter to our bugtracker. You can find the binaries in the usual spot on our download sites.

Have fun!
