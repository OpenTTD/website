--- title: OpenTTD 1.4.2-RC1 author: frosch --- The hunt for bugs in OpenTTD 1.4 continues. Today you can get your hands on the first release candidate for 1.4.2.

Most noticeable it works around issues with very recent versions of OSX resp. its video drivers; but it also makes games with multiple thousands of stations work again.

Please help testing OpenTTD 1.4.2-RC1 and report issues to our bug tracker.

Thanks, and have fun!
