Donate to OpenTTD

If you enjoy playing OpenTTD and would like to help support its development, please consider donating. OpenTTD is developed and released free of charge, and the developers give up their own free time to work on the game. In addition the server used to build the releases and host the web services costs a lot of money to run, so any support would be appreciated.

If you would like to support OpenTTD, donations can be made in a number of ways:

  • PayPal
    PayPal is the most common and perhaps the easiest way to make a donation. You can specify an amount to donate, in any currency supported by PayPal, and if you don't have a PayPal account, you can pay by credit/debit card without signing up for an account. If you would like to donate in this way, please click one of the following links:
    • Donate in British Pounds (GBP)

  • Cheque
    Donations by cheque are also a possibility within the United Kingdom. Outside of the UK, hefty charges may be made by our bank for cashing the cheque, so please contact Owen Rudge for more advice.
  • Other methods
    If you have another method by which you would like to donate, please contact Owen Rudge.
